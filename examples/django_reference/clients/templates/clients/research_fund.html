About the chart: Both the fund and its benchmark are indexed to 100 at the start of the selected timeframe — the line that climbs higher returned more over the window. Hover anywhere to see indexed values, raw NAV, and exact date for both lines. Volatility is the annualised standard deviation of daily log-returns (σ × √252).

Benchmark mapping: Large Cap → Nifty 50 · Mid Cap → Nifty Midcap 150 · Small Cap → Nifty Smallcap 250 · every other equity scheme (Multi-cap, Flexi, ELSS, Sectoral, etc.) → Nifty 500. Debt, hybrid and commodity funds get no benchmark line because comparing them to an equity index would mislead. Indices are sourced via well-tracking index funds on mfapi.in (tracking error ~0.05%), not scraped from NSE — same fetch path, same 12-hour cache, no extra dependency.
